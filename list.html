Optional resources: 

Research and read the following resources for your own enrichment:

  • Human Development Reports: The Human Development Report highlights the inequalities in human development and the impact this has on the achievement of the SDGs.

  • Climate Risk and Response: The McKinsey climate risk and socio-economic report examines the physical effects of the changing climate and seeks to understand the associated socio-economic impact.

  • Sustainability and diversity: The importance of, and connection between, sustainability, diversity and inclusion in businesses.

  • Inequality: A persisting challenge and its implications: The McKinsey report highlights the different areas of inequality, including wealth, income, consumption and opportunity.

  • Inside China’s Greenwashing: This article explores the Belt and Road Initiative, China’s strategy to combat climate change and “greenwashing”.
