1. Introduction

Learning outcomes: 

LO1: Discuss the sustainability-related risks and opportunities that businesses are currently facing and the commercial imperative for sustainability.

LO2: Show how businesses can create shared value through their operations and supply chain.

LO3: Illustrate how sustainability initiatives act as a driver for innovation and competitive advantage.

Businesses are increasingly becoming aware of the risks that sustainability challenges pose to their operations, along with the opportunities for long-term resilience presented by addressing these risks.

These challenges are not only numerous, but interconnected: economic, societal, geopolitical, technological, and environmental. Many are largely a consequence of the systems pressures and trends discussed in Module 1.
